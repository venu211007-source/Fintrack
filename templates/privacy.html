Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

FinTrack is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your financial data belongs to you.

What we collect
  • Your name and email address (for your account)
  • Bank statement data you upload (transactions, amounts, dates)
  • Account information you manually enter (bank name, currency)
What we do NOT do
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not connect to your bank directly
  • We do not share your financial information with advertisers
  • We do not use your data for any purpose other than showing it back to you
How your data is stored

Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (US West). Uploaded statement files are processed immediately and deleted from the server — only the extracted transaction records are saved to your account.

Security

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt. All connections use HTTPS encryption. We do not store your bank credentials or passwords.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings. Contact us at the email below if you need assistance.
